The first Three steps of meditation 💞

Step One

Take a seat: Find place to sit that feels calm and quiet to you.

Step Two

Set a time limit: If you're just beginning, it can help to choose a short time, such as five or 10 minutes.

Step Three

Notice Your body: You can sit in a chair with your feet on the floor, you can sit loosley cross-legged, you can kneel, all are fine. Just make sure you are stable and in position you can stay in for a while
