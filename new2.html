The Next Three steps of meditation 💞

Step Four

Feel your breath: Find place to sit that feels calm and quiet to you.

Step Five

Notice when your mind has wandered: Inevitably, your attention will leave the breath and wander to other places. When you get around to noticing that your mind has wandered—in a few seconds, a minute, five minutes—simply return your attention to the breath..

Step Six

Notice Your body: You can sit in a chair with your feet on the floor, you can sit loosley cross-legged, you can kneel, all are fine. Just make sure you are stable and in position you can stay in for a while
